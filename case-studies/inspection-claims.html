Inspection & Claims Flow

Creating a structured inspection and claims process that protects both hosts and guests — replacing a chaotic email-based system with a guided, photo-documented workflow.

−18% claims volume $ 500K annual savings
Before vs. After

Before

The email chain nightmare

Claims were submitted via email with loosely attached photos. No standard format, no required documentation, no timeline enforcement.

Support agents spent an average of 45 minutes per claim just organizing evidence. Disputes often came down to "he said, she said" because there was no documented vehicle condition at pickup.

Hosts were frustrated. Guests felt accused. Neither side had a clear, fair process.

After

Structured, documented, fair

Both host and guest complete guided photo inspections at pickup and return. The app walks them through required angles — exterior front, rear, sides, interior, odometer — with timestamps and GPS.

When a claim is filed, the system auto-generates a side-by-side comparison of pickup vs. return photos. Support agents see evidence immediately. Resolution time dropped from 12 days to 3.

Because both parties document condition, frivolous claims dropped dramatically. Hosts trust the process. Guests feel protected.

Design Details

Guided photo capture

Instead of "upload photos of your RV," the inspection flow shows a silhouette overlay for each required angle. Users align their camera to the guide, ensuring consistent, comparable documentation. This solved the #1 problem with the old system: inconsistent or missing evidence.

Mutual accountability

Both host and guest must complete the inspection. If a guest skips the pickup inspection, they can't dispute pre-existing damage later. If a host skips the return inspection, they lose claim eligibility for that trip. This mutual requirement cut bad-faith claims by over 40%.

Resolution timeline

Claims have a strict 72-hour window and clear status progression visible to both parties. Transparency killed the anxiety of "what's happening with my claim?" and reduced follow-up support contacts by 60%.

Less drama, more trust.
